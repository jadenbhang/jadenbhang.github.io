About us

Welcome to Cutie Cafe, a family-owned haven where every sip is a celebration of sweetness and every moment is infused with charm! Established with love and passion, our story began as a dream within the heart of our family. Inspired by generations of culinary traditions and a shared love for creating joy through delightful experiences, Cutie Cafe emerged as a warm, inviting space that reflects our commitment to quality and connection.

Founded by the Bhang family, our cafe is a testament to the values of warmth, togetherness, and the joy found in sharing a good cup of coffee. From the carefully selected beans to the meticulous crafting of pastries, every aspect of Cutie Cafe is a result of our family's dedication to creating a space that feels like an extension of home. We take pride in blending our heritage with modern charm, creating a unique atmosphere that welcomes everyone into the embrace of our family's legacy.

As a family-owned establishment, our commitment goes beyond serving delicious treats. It's about fostering a sense of community, where every visitor becomes an honorary member of our extended family. We invite you to be a part of our journey, to taste the love that goes into each creation, and to experience the warmth of a family-owned cafe that believes in turning simple moments into cherished memories.

Thank you for joining us on this exciting chapter of our story. Whether you're here for the first time or returning as a cherished regular, we extend our heartfelt welcome to Cutie Cafe – where family, flavor, and cuteness come together in perfect harmony.
